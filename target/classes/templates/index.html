Our mission

MyTreePal's mission is to create empathy for trees among the next generation, a vital environmental resource, through direct action and education.

Our vision

MyTreePal's vision is to build a green future through the future generations, which will not only provide them with clean air to breath but also a hospitable environment, by creating empathy and planting more trees.
